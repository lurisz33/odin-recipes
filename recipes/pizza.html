Classic stone oven pizza

The below recipe is my trusted way of making a delicious pizza in the stone oven

Overview

  • Total time: 3 days
  • Active time: 2 hours

Ingredients for 4 dough pieces

  • 680 of wheat type00
  • 440 of water
  • 4g of wet yeast
  • 16g of kosher salt

Preparation

  1. Mix the yeast with cold water and stir until dissolved
  2. Mix the wheat with the salt
  3. Slowly add the water to the flour and knead well
  4. Knead until good structure of the dough (window test)
  5. Store covered in a cool place for at least 48 hours
  6. Knead into balls of 280g about 6 hours before use and store again in cool place
  7. Remove dough balls about 30min before use depending on outside temperature
  8. Make great Pizza
